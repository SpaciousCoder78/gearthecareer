Key Skills needed for a Career as a Chartered Accountant are:

  1. Critical Thinking
  2. Budget Management
  3. SAP and SAP FICO
  4. Financial Analysis
  5. Evaluation and Management Auditing
  6. Tax Consulting and Auditing and many more..

Career Opportunities in Chartered Accountancy are:

  1. Accounting Clerks
  2. Chartered Accountants Public Accountant
  3. Treasurers and Controllers
  4. Cost Accountant
  5. Financial Analyst and Advisor
  6. Financial Managers and many more..
